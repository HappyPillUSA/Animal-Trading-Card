Giraffe

The Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth!Their legs alone are taller than many humans.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis ssp. peralta.
  • Weight: Up to 1,300 kilograms.
  • Size: Up to 6 meters tall.
  • Lifespan: Up to 25 years in the wild.

They can run as fast as 35 miles an hour over short distances, or cruise at 10 mph over longer distances.A giraffe's neck is too short to reach the ground.As a result, it has to awkwardly spread its front legs or kneel to reach the ground for a drink of water.Giraffes only need to drink once every few days. Most of their water comes from all the plants they eat, and they only need 5 to 30 minutes of sleep in a 24-hour period!
